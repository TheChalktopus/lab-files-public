Key Features

This box contains two LM YN 4-Channel solid state relays. The relays require 5 V, and each have four (3.3 V or 5 V) trigger signal inputs and four corresponding dry node switch outputs.

In the box, we have configured the both relays and their switch outputs to be powered off the same 5 V input, for use with these shutters https://github.com/MIT-CUA/mini_shutter. On each switch we have added a flyback diode allowing the this box to be used with inductive loads.

The front panel of the box is arranged such that each adjacent pair of isolating and non-isolating BNC connectors connects to the trigger signal input and corresponding switch on the relay.

Waterjet + Box Assembly instructions

Waterjet the front panel. Due to the length of the front panel, the right and left sides needed to be waterjet one at a time. The waterjet files are located in the FrAg Box: \FrAg\CAD\Electronics\CAD\Electronics\Shutter Box\Waterjet

  • Front Panel-2importedDXFright.dxf
  • Front Panel-2importedDXFright.omx
  • Front Panel-2importedDXFleft.dxf
  • Front Panel-2importedDXFleft.omx
  • Front Panel-2.DXF

Waterjet the two blocks for the relays to sit on. The waterjet files are located in the FrAg Box: \FrAg\CAD\Electronics\CAD\Electronics\Shutter Box\Waterjet

  • blockimportedDXF.dxf
  • blockimportedDXF.omx

Tap all holes for 4-40.

Assemble the box according to the box's assembly manual.

Epoxy the two blocks to the bottom of the box and screw down the relays onto the nylon spacers.

Electrical assembly instructions

  1. Connect to DC+ and DC- on each relay to the banana connectors on the front panel.
  2. Connect the eight non-isolating BNC connectors to X1-4 on each.
  3. Ground the box itself and connect the ground of the non-isolating BNC connectors to XM.
  4. Short DC+ to on of the screw terminals for each of the switches.
  5. Connect the other screw terminal on each switch to the isolating BNC connectors
  6. Connect the ground of each of the isolating BNC connectors to DC- on the relay
  7. Add the diodes recommended in figures 5 and 6 in the relay manual

Front Panel

The front panel contains

  • x2 banana jacks (+5V, GND)
  • 8 isolating BNC panel mounts
  • 8 non-isolating BNC panel mounts
